History

The Journey to Trophi

From a simple school project to a modern offline-first food inventory application.

2022 — Cummy

The first version was developed as the final project for my Professional Course in Computer Systems Management and Programming. Built with Java and XML for Android 8.0, it included manual food management and local storage, earning a final grade of 17/20.

Lessons Learned

Cummy highlighted several limitations, including manual product entry, a basic user interface, unencrypted storage, and limited scalability. These challenges inspired a complete redesign.

2026 — Trophi

Trophi rebuilds the original idea from the ground up with an offline-first architecture, barcode scanning, OCR, recipe recommendations, dietary filters, stronger privacy, and a modern mobile experience.

About

Why Trophi?

Trophi was created to help people take control of their kitchen in a simple, private and efficient way. Instead of relying on cloud services, all your information stays securely on your device.

By combining barcode scanning, expiration tracking and recipe suggestions, Trophi helps reduce food waste while making everyday meal planning easier.
